Getting to know us

My name is Vivian Evans and i run Fun 2 Learn Chilminding Service, in Weston-Super-Mare. I am Ofsted registered and i have a Peadiatric First Aid certificate. We love to go out somewhere every day. There is a park round the corner, also the beach and woods are close by. My aim is to provide a safe caring environment with the emphasis on fun. I provide home cooked meals.

I have three children of my own, two grandchildren and a wonderful dog. I also have experience of dealing with asthma, learning difficulties and challenging behaviour.
