Activities

Childminder Groups

We attend one of the local childminder groups each week. These are usually run at childrens centers. These are stimulating environments which offer fun activities, stories, songs and interaction with other children and adults.

Park

There is a small park round the corner.

Garden

When the weather is nice we can be in the garden.

Beach

Weston has a nice sandy beach. There is also rockpools to explore. A fun bus ride to
Sand Bay reveals hills to climb and a different beach to explore.
