Where to go for help

Ofsted - Visit Ofsted - All childminders are inspected on a regular basis. You are able to check on childminder inspection reports and contact them with queries
Social Care - e-mail: childcare.weston@nsomerset.gov.uk, Local council organises training for childminders and information for parents.
NCMA - National childminding association offers help and information to parents.
FIS - Family Information Service holds lists of childminders, nurseries and playgroups for your local area. All fees and policies are reviewed yearly in September. We carry out risk assessments for all outings and repeat regularly for house and garden.
